The Characters

Shinnosuke Nohara (Shinchan)

The mischievous 5-year-old protagonist known for his hilarious antics and love of choco chips.

Himawari (Sister)

The youngest of the Noharas. Loves shiny objects and brand-name goods.

Toru Kazama

The smart and responsible friend of Shinchan, often the voice of reason.

Misae Nohara (Mom)

Shinchan's short-tempered but loving mother, always trying to keep him in line.

Hiroshi Nohara (Dad)

The laid-back father who works at a company and enjoys his evening beer.

Shiro (Family Dog)

The family dog — small, fluffy, and sometimes the most sensible member.
